# House Style

Prefer clear editorial motion over decorative complexity.

- Use restrained typography and strong hierarchy.
- Keep text readable within a 3-5 second viewing window.
- Use motion to reveal argument structure, not to decorate.
- Avoid web-dashboard clutter, tiny labels, pie charts, and dense axes.
- Use concise cards, large numbers, progress bars, contrast blocks, or quote frames.

Default visual mood: modern Chinese business explainer, high contrast, clean data
storytelling, suitable for finance/report/book commentary.
