# Visual Styles

## data_driven

Clean business-data style. Neutral background, high-contrast type, restrained
accent colors, large numeric hierarchy, subtle grid or guide lines.

## light_corporate

Bright report style. White or light gray background, dark text, blue/green/red
accent colors, soft shadows, professional presentation feel.

## dark_premium

Dark editorial style. Deep neutral background, warm or cyan accents, low-noise
glows, strong title and card hierarchy.

## cinematic_contrast

High-contrast filmic style. Black/white foundation, one warning accent color,
large typographic impact, hard cuts and decisive reveals.

## historical_woodblock

Historical woodblock print and traditional Chinese editorial style.
- Background: Deep charcoal black (#121212), resembling soot ink.
- Typography: Soft cream off-white (#EAE5D9) for text and titles. Use serif fallback styling (Songti / SimSun) for a printed press look.
- Accents: Crimson cinnabar red (#B32424) for warning text, key metrics, and heavy solid frames.
- Layout: Strong vertical typography lines, thick solid borders (resembling traditional woodcut block borders), or calligraphic scroll spacing.
- Motion: Decisive ink-sweep reveals, scroll slide-ins, and direct fade transitions. Avoid digital glow effects, high-tech grid-lines, or floating particle systems.
